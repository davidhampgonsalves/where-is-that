Wondering where Brunei is located?

East Asia & Pacific is where Brunei is located Brunei at Latitude: 4.826 and Longitude: 115.207 . Brunei Darussalam is a Sovereign country. It uses the Brunei dollar as its primary currency. The nation's abbreviated name is Brunei and its postal identifier is BN.

Brunei is comprised of Brunei and Muara, Temburong, Belait, Tutong.
