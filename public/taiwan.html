Where is
Taiwan?

Find out where Taiwan is located?

It is a Sovereign country. The state's abbreviated(two character) name is Taiwan and its postal identifier is TW.

Taiwan is comprised of Kaohsiung, Tainan, Chiayi city, Keelung City, Taichung City, Hualien, Yunlin, Kaohsiung City, Taichung, Taitung, Changhwa, Pingtung, Tainan City, Hsinchu city, Ilan, Taipei City, Taoyuan, Hsinchu, Taipei, Miaoli, Chiayi, Chinmen, Penghu.
