Have you ever wondered where Burkina Faso is located?

Sub-Saharan Africa is where Burkina Faso is located Burkina Faso at Latitude: 15.003 and Longitude: -0.426 . The state's abbreviated(two character) name is B.F. and its postal identifier is BF. The country has a female population of 8,542.51 compared to its male population of 8,425.34. Burkina Faso's maternal mortality rate is 560 deaths per 1 000 000 live births. Burkina Faso has recieved a gender equality index of 0.596 which is bellow average. The nation has revieved a human development index of 0.331. The minority of the population of Burkina Faso lives in urban centers which is typical of the region. 3% of all species that reside in Burkina Faso are considered to be endangered. Burkina Faso uses the CFA franc as its primary currency. It produces 0.1 tons of carbon dioxide annually derived from agriculture, transportation and factories. The state is a Sovereign country. Burkina Faso spends 3.4% of its annual GDP on public health care which has resulted in a health index of 0.559 and a life life expectancy of 55.4 years. Burkina Faso's forrest regions cover 5,769 thousand hectares of the country.
