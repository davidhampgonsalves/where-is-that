Where is
Tuvalu?

Want to discover where Tuvalu is located?

East Asia & Pacific is where Tuvalu is located. 15% of all species that reside in Tuvalu are considered to be endangered which is quite high. Tuvalu's abbreviated(two character) name is Tuv. and its postal identifier is TV. The majority of the population of Tuvalu lives in urban centers which is typical of the region. Tuvalu uses the Australian dollar as its primary currency. Tuvalu's forrest regions cover 1 thousand hectares of the country. The country is a Sovereign country.
