Have you ever wondered where Libya is located?

Middle East & North Africa is where Libya is located Libya at Latitude: 33.092 and Longitude: 11.757 . The country is a Sovereign country. It uses the Libyan dinar as its primary currency. Socialist People's Libyan Arab Jamahiriya's abbreviated(two character) name is Libya and its postal identifier is LY.

Libya is comprised of An Nuqat al Khams, Tarhunah, Benghazi, Al Butnan, Murzuq, Misratah, Mizdah, Sabha, Zlitan, Al Jifarah, Al Hizam Al Akhdar, Al Jufrah, Az Zawiyah, Yafran-Jadu, Surt, Al Kufrah, Ash Shati', Tajura' wa an Nawahi al Arba, Al Marqab, Ghat, Bani Walid, Al Jabal al Akhdar, Ajdabiya, Ghadamis, Al Qubbah.
