Sub-Saharan Africa is where Lesotho is located Lesotho at Latitude: -28.7 and Longitude: 28.759 . Lesotho has revieved a human development index of 0.45. The minority of the population of Lesotho lives in urban centers which is typical of the region. It spends 3.6% of its annual GDP on public health care which has resulted in a health index of 0.445 and a life life expectancy of 48.2 years. Lesotho has a female population of 1,113.44 compared to its male population of 1,080.41. It uses the Lesotho loti as its primary currency. The country's maternal mortality rate is 530 deaths per 1 000 000 live births. Lesotho's forrest regions cover 44 thousand hectares of the country. 3% of all species that reside in Lesotho are considered to be endangered. It has recieved a gender equality index of 0.532 which is bellow average. Kingdom of Lesotho is a Sovereign country. It's abbreviated name is Les. and its postal identifier is LS.

Lesotho is comprised of Maseru, Quthing, Leribe, Qacha's Nek, Mohale's Hoek, Thaba-Tseka, Berea, Mokhotlong, Butha-Buthe, Mafeteng.
